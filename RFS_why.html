"What is ROLL FOR SONG ?"

ROLL FOR SONG - “Let the Dice be the DJ”

Roll For Song is an interactive live music live-action game that can be played by everyone at the gig! With the many rolls of the dice, let luck choose the SONG, STYLE, and the KEY. Once the musician(s) begin, the interactivity begins with the MID-SONG MODIFIERS! Let the dice call for a drum breakdown, an instrument switch, or a nifty trick! We never know which way it will go and it never happens the same way twice!

Why Do We Play Roll For Song

  1. It’s easier for someone else (or something else) to choose for you.
  2. It’s a fun way to get interactive with the musicians.
  3. It’s fun to roll dice and listen to fun music.

Dice/Coin Used for This Version of RFS:

  • Standard Dice: 1D4, 1D6, 1D8 (numbered 1-8)
  • “SigCoin”: oversized coin with #/b sides
  • “KeySig” Dice: 1D4, 1D6, 1D8 (numbered 0-7)

(Any coin can replace the “SigCoin” by assigning the “Heads” to “Flats” and the “Tails” to “Sharps”, or vice versa just keep track)

(And any normal 1D4, 1D6, 1D8 can replace the “KeySig” Dice by assigning the highest number on the die to “0”, corresponding with “C”)
