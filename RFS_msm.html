What Are Mid-Song Modifiers?

These rolls can AND SHOULD happen while we are playing the game. Come talk to us and roll the dice to see what crazy wrenches you can throw into the music!

  1. 1st. Roll the Opal 1D6 to choose WHO will be modified:

    • (1) or (4) is Shawn
    • (2) or (5) is Ron
    • (3) or (6) is Gordon

  2. 2nd. Roll the Opal 1D4 to choose HOW they will be modified:

    Shawn Ron Gordon
    1. Change Shirt 1. Tap Dance 1. Stand On Bass
    2. Switch to Sax 2. Switch to Clarinet 2. Reverse Left Hand
    3. Start Singing 3. Start Singing 3. Start Singing
    4. Play Percussion 4. Play Percussion 4. Play Percussion
