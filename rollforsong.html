"PlayGuide for ROLL FOR SONG"

How to Play Roll for Song

  1. Roll for Song

    1. Roll the Opal 1D8 to choose the SONG we play:
      1. When the Saints
      2. All of Me
      3. Coquette
      4. Summertime
      5. L-O-V-E
      6. St. Thomas
      7. My Little Suede Shoes
      8. Bésame Mucho

  2. Roll for Style

    1. Roll the Opal 1D4 to choose the STYLE of the SONG:
      1. 2 Beat
      2. Latin
      3. Funky
      4. Jazz

  3. Roll for Key — Use the “Roll For Key” Slider Sleeve

    1. Flip the SIG COIN to choose the Key Signature:
      1. Sharps (the hash tag)
      2. Flats (the lower case b)
    2. Roll 1 of the 3 white dice to choose how many Accidentals:
      1. Easy Keys - 1D4 - 74% of all music
      2. Hard Keys - 1D6 - 25% of all music
      3. Stupid Keys - 1D8 - less than 1% of all music
    3. Match Key Signature to Accidentals to determine key:
      • Easy Keys:
        • -. b / #
        • 0. C / C
        • 1. F / G
        • 2. Bb / D
        • 3. Eb / A
      • Hard Keys:
        • 4. Ab / E
        • 5. Db / B
      • Stupid Keys:
        • 6. Gb / F#
        • 7. Cb / C#
