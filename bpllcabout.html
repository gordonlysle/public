GORDON BASH

Owner

Gordon is a bandleader, composer, booker, and bassist who has toured all over the world. (AL1CE, Alice Underground, American Authors, BASHROCK, Colin Cunningham, Corky Halle, Dave Grohl, Diana Krall, Duluth Superior Symphony Orchestra, Eru Matsumoto, Jason Sudeikis, Kerry Ellis, Leslie Cours Mather, Magnolia Memoir, Marty & Elayne, Mankind is Obsolete, Margaret Cho, Mortified, Page Cavannaugh, Save Ferris, Seth MacFarlane, Sting, "Weird Al" Yankovic, Wild Honey Orchestra, William Close & the Earth Harp Collective, Willie McNeil, Yoko Takahasi) . He has been performing and booking at the Sunset Tower Hotel since 2005.
